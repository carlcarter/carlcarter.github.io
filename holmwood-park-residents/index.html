What Does It Mean to Be a Member?

Holmwood Residents Limited is responsible for managing our estate. Right now, external companies are making **critical decisions** on our behalf, including service charges, maintenance, and estate management.

🔹 Why Should You Become a Member?

By becoming a member, you gain the legal right to:

  • Vote on important decisions regarding estate management and service charges.
  • Hold property managers accountable for fair pricing and transparency.
  • Ensure that residents, not external firms, control the management of our estate.
  • Reduce unnecessary management fees and improve service quality.

🏡 What Happens Without Resident Control?

Without resident involvement, external management can:

  • Increase service charges without justification.
  • Hire expensive contractors without consulting residents.
  • Ignore maintenance issues or delay essential repairs.
  • Refuse to disclose financial details of our service charges.

By becoming a member, you ensure that decisions are made in the best interests of the residents.

📜 Who Qualifies for Membership?

Membership is available to:

  • ✅ **Homeowners who hold a freehold interest** within the estate.
  • ✅ **Leaseholders who own long leases** (e.g., 99+ years).
  • ✅ **Housing association leaseholders** (if explicitly permitted).

❌ **Who does NOT qualify?**

  • 🚫 Short-term tenants who rent from landlords.
  • 🚫 Leaseholders with temporary rental agreements.
