What Does It Mean to Be a Member?

Holmwood Residents Limited is responsible for managing our estate. Right now, external companies are making critical decisions on our behalf, including service charges, maintenance, and estate management.

🔹 Why Should You Become a Member?

🗳 Vote on Important Decisions

Have a **direct say** in estate management, service charges, and key financial decisions.

⚖ Hold Property Managers Accountable

Ensure **fair pricing, transparency**, and prevent unnecessary increases in service charges.

🏡 Residents Control the Estate

Prevent external firms from making decisions without **resident input**.

💰 Reduce Management Fees

Lower service charges and ensure **better quality services** with direct oversight.

📜 Who Qualifies for Membership?

✅ Freehold Homeowners

Homeowners/Unitholders who hold a freehold interest within the estate qualify for membership.
