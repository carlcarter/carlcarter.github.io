Holmwood Park Residents

Join us in taking an active role in our estate's management.

Become a Member

To become a member and gain voting rights, copy and submit this letter:

    Dear [Company Name],
    I, [Your Name] residing at [Your Address], request membership in Holmwood Residents Limited.
    As a qualifying homeowner, I would like to be recognized as a voting member.
    Sincerely,  
    [Your Name]

Apply to Be a Director

If you wish to join the board, submit the following letter:

Dear [Company Name],
I, [Your Name] of [Your Address], would like to put myself forward as a director of Holmwood Residents Limited.
I believe my experience in [Your Expertise] will be beneficial to the community.
Best Regards,  
[Your Name]

Request a List of Members

To ensure transparency, you can request the list of existing members:

Dear [Company Name],
I formally request a copy of the register of members under my rights as a shareholder of Holmwood Residents Limited.
Please provide this in line with the Companies Act 2006.
Sincerely,  
[Your Name]

Contact us at [Your Email] for any questions.
