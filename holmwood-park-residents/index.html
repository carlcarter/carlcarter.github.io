🚨 What Is This All About?

If you own a property in Holmwood Park, you are automatically a potential member of Holmwood Residents Ltd. In the company’s terms, you’re a “Unitholder” – meaning you hold the lease or freehold for a home on the estate. As a Unitholder, you have the right to become a member of the residents’ company. By joining, you gain a voice in how our community is managed. This site explains your rights, the benefits of joining, and how to sign up.

Holmwood Residents Limited is the company responsible for managing our estate, yet residents currently have little to no say in how key decisions are made.

At present, Trinity Estates - a third-party management company - holds control over our estate’s financial decisions, policies, and maintenance contracts. As residents, we are paying for services that we have no control over, and many of us have noticed issues with the way our estate is being managed.

⚠ Neglected Repairs

Residents have reported broken speed humps that have been fixed poorly, while essential maintenance is delayed.

💸 Unnecessary Spending

Money has been spent on painting new road markings and 20mph speed signs—changes that nobody appears to have requested.

🚫 No Consultation

These decisions impact our community, yet they are made without our input or transparency.

Without resident oversight, we risk further unnecessary spending, rising service charges, and poor management that does not reflect our needs or priorities. The more members we have in Holmwood Residents Limited, the stronger our collective voice will be.

If we don’t take action, who will? Now is the time to step forward and take control of the place we call home.

🔹 Why Should You Become a Member?

Your Rights: Every Holmwood Park homeowner has the right to apply for membership in Holmwood Residents Ltd. This membership isn’t automatic – you need to apply – but the company’s rules guarantee that any qualifying homeowner can join. Once you’re a member, you have legal rights in the company’s governance, including voting at meetings and accessing company information.

Voting Power in Decisions

Members can vote on important decisions at annual general meetings or through written resolutions. You’ll have a say in budgets, policies, and which managing agents or contractors are hired.

Community Decision-Making

With more members, our community gains control. Rather than an outside company deciding everything, we as residents can collectively determine how to maintain and improve our estate.

Financial Transparency

As a member, you can access the company’s accounts and see exactly how your estate charges are being used. No more mysteries – you can help ensure money is spent wisely on the park, roads, lighting, and other services you care about.

Accountability for Managers

Holmwood Residents Ltd can appoint or dismiss the property management company. When homeowners are in charge, the managing agent answers to us. If the managing agent isn’t performing or costs are unreasonable, members can push for changes. In short, you can hold property managers accountable through the residents’ company.

Protecting Your Investment

Having control means protecting the value of your home. You and your neighbors will make decisions that prevent neglect or excessive fees that could hurt property values. It’s local stewardship by the people who live here and care most.

Building Community

Working together with your neighbors through the company builds a stronger community. You’ll meet fellow residents, share ideas, and collaborate to make Holmwood Park an even better place to live.

Overall, joining Holmwood Residents Ltd empowers you with information and influence that non-member homeowners won’t have.

It’s your estate – membership ensures you have a voice in its future.

What Is Expected of Members?

We want to clarify what’s expected of a member and what is not to make it clear that membership does not require a significant time commitment.

✅ Legal Right To Vote

As a member of Holmwood Residents Limited, you will have the legal right to vote on key decisions affecting our estate.

✅ Receive Updates

You will receive updates and notifications about important issues and upcoming votes.

✅ Take Part In General Meetings

Your primary responsibility is to participate in General Meetings, which occur annually or as needed when key decisions must be made.

🚫 Meeting Expectation

You are NOT required to attend every meeting—voting can often be done by proxy if you are unable to participate in person.

🚫 No Day to Day Management

You don’t need to get involved in day-to-day management—your role is simply to have a say in how the estate is run.

🚫 Directorship

You are NOT required to take on a directorial role—becoming a director is a separate voluntary role.

📜 Who Qualifies for Membership?

✅ Freehold Homeowners

Homeowners who own a property on the estate qualify for membership.

✅ Leasehold Shared Ownership Homeowners

Leaseholders who own a part of a property on the estate qualify for membership.

🚫 Renters

Unfortunately, tenants renting from a landlord, including social housing/housing association landlords, do not qualify for membership.

You can still raise your queries and requests to your landlord instead.

📩 How to Become a Member?

It’s simple!

  1. 📂 Gather Evidence

    Ensure you qualify for membership by proving ownership.

    If you're a freeholder, obtain a copy of your title deed registration from the Land Registry.

    Leaseholders may need to provide a copy of their lease agreement.

  2. 📝 Write a Membership Request Letter

    Send a formal membership application letter to Holmwood Residents Limited, requesting to be added as a member.

    This letter must include:

    ✅ Your full name

    ✅ Your property address

    ✅ Whether you are a freeholder or leaseholder

    📜 View Sample Membership Request Letter

  3. 📝 Fill Out the Membership Application

    Complete a formal Membership Application Form for Holmwood Residents Limited.

    Simply download and fill in the sample form with your details.

    📜 View Sample Membership Application

  4. 📬 Send Your Application by Registered Post

    Now you should have three documents ready:

    📄 Proof of eligibility (Step 1)

    📜 Membership Request Letter (Step 2)

    📝 Completed Membership Application Form (Step 3)

    Send all documents via registered post to Holmwood Residents Limited at the address detailed in the sample letter.

  5. ✅ Receive Confirmation

    Once your request has been processed, Holmwood Residents Limited should confirm your membership and update the official register of members.

    If you do not receive confirmation within a reasonable timeframe, be sure to follow up to ensure your request has been processed.

  6. ✅ Get Involved

    That’s it – you’re officially a member! There’s no ongoing paperwork after that. Now you can attend Annual General Meetings, vote on issues, or even stand for election to the Holmwood Residents Ltd board of directors if you wish. Of course, participation is up to you, but we encourage all new members to stay engaged. Even simply casting your vote or voicing your opinion on key issues will make a difference.
