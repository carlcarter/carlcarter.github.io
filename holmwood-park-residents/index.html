Empowering homeowners to take control of our estate

What Does It Mean to Be a Member?

Holmwood Residents Limited is responsible for managing the estate. By becoming a member, you gain a say in how the estate is run.

Benefits of Being a Member

Voting Rights – Influence decisions affecting the estate. Transparency – Ensure fair handling of service charges and contracts. Control Over Management – Elect directors who represent residents. Financial Oversight – Prevent excessive management fees.

Who Can Join?

Membership is open to:

  • All private homeowners (leasehold or freehold)
  • Housing association leaseholders (where applicable)

Renters do not qualify but can support by encouraging their landlords to join.

How to Take Control of Our Estate

Submit a request to join as a voting member.

Step 2: Apply to Become a Director

Once you are a member, apply to be a director.

[Your Name]

As a member, you can request the list of all current members.

Sincerely,  
[Your Name]
