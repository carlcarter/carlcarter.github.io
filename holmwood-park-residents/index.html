Holmwood Park Residents

Empowering homeowners to take control of our estate

How to Take Action

Step 1: Become a Member

Submit a request to join as a voting member of the estate.

Step 2: Apply as a Director

Once you're a member, you can apply to become a director.

Step 3: Request the Members List

Request the member list to ensure transparency and engagement.

Step 4: Engage with Residents

Build community support and ensure residents are informed.

Step 5: Participate in Decision-Making

Work with other members to shape the future of the estate.

Become a Member +

To become a member and gain voting rights, copy the letter below:

Dear [Company Name],
I, [Your Name], residing at [Your Address], request membership in Holmwood Residents Limited.
As a qualifying homeowner, I would like to be recognized as a voting member.
Sincerely,  
[Your Name]

Apply to Be a Director +

If you wish to join the board, copy and send this letter:

Dear [Company Name],
I, [Your Name], of [Your Address], would like to put myself forward as a director of Holmwood Residents Limited.
I believe my experience in [Your Expertise] will be beneficial to the community.
Best Regards,  
[Your Name]

Request a List of Members +

You can request the register of members using this template:

Dear [Company Name],
I formally request a copy of the register of members under my rights as a shareholder of Holmwood Residents Limited.
Please provide this in line with the Companies Act 2006.
Sincerely,  
[Your Name]
